Who Are We?

Motivated by a shared love for the rhythmic crash of waves and the feeling of sand between our toes, our family decided to build our dream into reality. we yearned to create a space where others could escape the everyday hustle and reconnect with nature's raw beauty right on the coast. Inspired by the scenic hiking paths that wound through the nearby cliffs and down to the shore, and wanting a unique, comfortable lodging experience, we established "Pacific Trails Resort" offering cozy yurts just steps from the ocean. A perfect blend of seaside tranquility and coastal adventure.

Whats Important To Us?

At Pacific Trails Resort, environmental stewardship and exceptional guest experiences go hand-in-hand. Our family is deeply committed to minimizing our footprint, incorporating practices like solar power, water conservation, and protecting the delicate coastal flora surrounding the yurts. This dedication to sustainability enhances the connection to nature that defines a stay, allowing guests to relax knowing their visit supports responsible tourism.
