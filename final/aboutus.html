Who Are We?

  • Emily

  • Sally, and Jacob

Motivated by a shared love for the rhythmic crash of waves and the feeling of sand between our toes, our family decided to build our dream into reality. we yearned to create a space where others could escape the everyday hustle and reconnect with nature's raw beauty right on the coast. Inspired by the scenic hiking paths that wound through the nearby cliffs and down to the shore, and wanting a unique, comfortable lodging experience, we established "Pacific Trails Resort" offering cozy yurts just steps from the ocean.

Whats Important To Us?

Enhancing the guest experience at Pacific Trails Resort extends far beyond the comfort of the yurts, focusing on immersive engagement with the stunning coastal environment. Discover hidden gems and breathtaking vistas on guided hikes along the very trails that inspired the resort's name, led by guides knowledgeable about local flora and fauna. Guests can also explore the dynamic shoreline from the water with kayaking adventures, or embrace tranquility through dedicated bird watching sessions, identifying the diverse avian species that frequent this rich Pacific habitat. These activities are designed to deepen each visitor's connection to the area, ensuring a memorable and enriching stay filled with discovery.
